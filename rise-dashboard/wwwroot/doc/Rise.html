How to Vote on the Rise network

Firstly to vote we go to

Wallet.rise.vision

 

Assumption that you already have a Rise wallet, click Existing account


Enter your wallet that you wish to vote/change your vote and press continue

We are going to want to have Full access to be able to vote so select the top option

At the top right of the screen there is a clog, click that and go into settings

In the above example I am currently voting for my delegate ’micoley’
click on the Voted delegate

 

You’ll be prompted to either enter the delegate name or address that you are looking for

I am going to vote for one of my pools that can be found in

https://dashboard.rise.vision/DelegateForms

(There will be another guide how to read the information that is in the Dashboard)

Baguette is one of my delegates

Notice above in the calculator I’ve entered in the number of Rise in my wallet, if I hit enter I’ll get daily Rise estimates for the delegates that I’m looking at (I should by paid approx 28.294 Rise daily)

In dollars at its current price that is $0.25 daily.

 

searching for ’baguette’ brings up the delegate that I want to vote for, being on https://dashboard.rise.vision/DelegateForms I know that this is currently a paying delegate.

Select ‘Cast Vote’


I am prompted for my 12 word mnemonic that I would’ve been given when I first created the wallet.

Enter in the passphase and you’ve voted!

 

There are a couple of places where you can confirm that the vote has taken place successfully

On explorer.rise.vision

In the latest transactions page

]under Latest Transactions, the vote should appear here after a few minutes

You can click into the transaction ID on the lefthand side (15779116686255473141)

At the bottom I can see that I am no longer voting for micoley and that the network is showing that I am now voting for baguette. If you have never voted before there will not be a deleted votes section but there will by the Added votes section to indicate that you are now voting for a delegate.

I can also go onto my wallet page aswell

(https://explorer.rise.vision/address/17109809254929634793R)

 

Under the votes sections if I expand I can see that I am now voting for baguette and right at the bottom I can see that my delegate vote is going through the 101 confirmations before I will officially be voting for baguette.

My delegate pays out daily at 6pm BST so I can wait until then before I get my first payment,

After a full 24 hour pay cycle, I’ve received just over 29 Rise from my baguette delegate that is triggered to pay daily just after 18:00 BST.

 

and thats all there is to voting and receiving reward.
